## ğŸ“– What is HandmadeExpo?

**HandmadeExpo** is an Android mobile application designed to bridge the gap between talented artisans who create handmade products and buyers who appreciate unique, handcrafted items. Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, HandmadeExpo introduces a **real-time bargaining system** that allows buyers to negotiate prices directly with sellers through an integrated chat feature.

Built as a school project using **Kotlin** and **Android Studio**, HandmadeExpo demonstrates a complete marketplace ecosystem with three user roles: **Buyers**, **Sellers**, and **Administrators**. The app enables sellers to list their handmade products, buyers to browse and make price offers, and admins to manage the entire platform through a dedicated panel.
